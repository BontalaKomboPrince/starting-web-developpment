Tim Berner-Lee

Inventor of HTML

TimBL, is an English engineer and computer scientist,
best known as the inventor of the World Wide Web.
He is currently a Professor of Computer Science at the
University of Oxford, UK. He made a proposal for an
information management system in March 1989, and he
implemented the first successful communication between
a Hypertext Transfer Protocol (HTTP) client and server
via the internet in mid-November the same year
